About Us

Haulcap was started by two college kids, Corey and Patrick, who wanted to buy a second-hand ping-pong table. With each posting reading "pick-up only" and unknowing of anyone with a pick-up truck, the order came to a hault. It was then, Corey spurted the idea of Haulcap to Patrick. There are countless people in cities without access to trucks, so why not put those people in contact with those who own one?

Why pay the money to rent a truck and be bothered with having to do the work yourself when you can use Haulcap? We eliminate this hassle through finding a truck and grabbing a helper for you. Haulcap was founded on the idea of connecting people across cities through completing a delivery service that is affordable, efficient, and effective.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions from customers

Q: What is Haulcap?
A: Haulcap is a single point of contact that connects customers to drivers who can deliver your items.

Q: How much does it cost?
A: Each hual has a flat fee of $25 and $0.50 per mile.

Q: What is Haulcap used for?
A: Haulcap is used to move large items purchased from Craigslist and retail stores like IKEA and Walmart that do not fit in your car.

Frequently asked questions from drivers



Q: How do I sign up?
A: Send us an email at Haulcap@gmail.com providing contact information to join our network of drivers.

Q: When do I get paid?
A: Like Uber, your rides will accumulate and at your discretion we will transfer you the funds.

Q: What kind of certifaction do I need?
A: Drivers need a valid license, proof of insurance, and pass a background check.
