FAQ

Frequently asked questions from customers



Q: What is Haulcap?
A: Haulcap is a single point of contact that connects customers to drivers who can deliver your items.

Q: How much does it cost?
A: Each item has a flat fee of $25 and $0.50 per mile. Each additoinal item is $15.

Q: What is Haulcap used for?
A: Haulcap is used to move large items purchased from Craigslist and retail stores like IKEA and Walmart that do not fit in your car.

Frequently asked questions from drivers



Q: How do I sign up?
A: Send us an email at Haulcap@gmail.com providing contact information to join our network of drivers.

Q: When do I get paid?
A: Like Uber, your rides will accumulate and at your discretion we will transfer you the funds.

Q: What kind of certifaction do I need?
A: Drivers need a valid license, proof of insurance, and pass a background check.
