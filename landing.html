About Us

Haulcap was started by two college kids, Corey and Patrick, who wanted to buy a second-hand ping-pong table. With each posting reading "pick-up only" and unknowing of anyone with a pick-up truck, the order came to a hault. It was then, Corey spurted the idea of Haulcap to Patrick. There are countless people in cities without access to trucks, so why not put those people in contact with those who own one?

Why pay the money to rent a truck and be bothered with having to do the work yourself when you can use Haulcap? We eliminate this hassle through finding a truck and grabbing a helper for you. Haulcap was founded on the idea of connecting people across cities through completing a delivery service that is affordable, efficient, and effective.
